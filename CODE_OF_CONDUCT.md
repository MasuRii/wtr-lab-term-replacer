# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Assume good intent when reviewing issues and pull requests.
- Accept maintainer decisions when a contribution is out of scope.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption
- Publishing private information without permission
- Spam or unrelated promotion

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.
