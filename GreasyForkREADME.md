# WTR Lab Term Replacer

Replace terms and names in WTR Lab chapters with your preferred wording.

## Installation

1. Install a userscript manager such as ScriptCat, Violentmonkey, Tampermonkey, or Stay.
2. Install the script from Greasy Fork: https://greasyfork.org/en/scripts/552945-wtr-lab-term-replacer
3. Visit WTR Lab and refresh any already-open matching pages after installation.

## Current Version

- Package: `wtr-lab-term-replacer-webpack`
- Version: `5.7.2`

## Usage

Open a WTR Lab chapter page and confirm the term replacement controls appear and saved replacements apply.
